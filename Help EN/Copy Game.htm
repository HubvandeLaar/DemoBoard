Copy Game

Copies the current game as PGN or XPGN to the clipbaord.

PGN: Standard format for most Chess programms. The .pgn is saved without the Tags 'Title', 'Memo', 'FENGraphicals' and field markers other than 'Red', 'Yellow' and 'Green' rounded rectangles and without the coloured texts.

XPGN: Contains extra elements created for DemoBoard. Includes the Tags 'Title, 'Memo', 'FENGraphicals' and fieldmarkers such as the 'Dot', 'Circle' and 'Rectangle'
