Export to PDF

Exports the 'Initial Diagrams' of each game into a PDF document.

The PDF can be printed and handed out to students as exercises or explanation of a specific topic.

The diagrams are positioned in different layouts:

  • In 12, 9, 6 or 4 diagrams per page.
  • With empty bottom text, a hint from the 'Title of the Memo', or the entire 'Memo Text'.
  • And bottom text in specified 'Font size'.

The origin of all elements:

  • The 'Diagram' is taken from the initial diagram of the game.
  • The 'Page Header' is taken from the PGN-filename.
  • The 'Title of the Memo' comes from 'TITLE', stored at the TAGs of the game.
  • The 'Memo Text' comes from the text stored at the 'MEMO' TAG of the game.
