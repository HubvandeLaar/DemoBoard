Entering Moves

Dragging a piece on the board generates a move on the ‘Move List’.

Since children also perform irregular moves and when discussing their game the actual situation should be presented, illegal moves also are possible. Also moving twice with the same color is accepted. Irregular moves are recorded in a long notation. Something that looks like castling is handled like castling.

Dragging graphical elements does not generate moves.
