Multiple Choice

It is also possible to have a Multiple-choice Quiz. The question is at the ‘Comment-before’ main-line move. The ‘Comment-before’ should contain the question ending with an “?”. The correct answer is the main-line move of the game. The other options are the sub variants of the main-line move.

When a Multiple-choice Quiz is opened, it quickly plays all moves towards the first question. At correct answer, gives a compliment, increases the score by 10 and auto-plays to the next question. At incorrect answers, gives some feedback and plays the variant moves until the end and returns to the beginning of the question.
